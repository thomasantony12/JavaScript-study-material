Variables

  • Cannot be a keyword. (eg: var, cons, console, log)

  • Should be meaning full.

  • Cannot start with a number. (eg: 1name)

  • Cannot contain space or hyphen. (eg: car-name or car name)

  • Cannot be a keyword(eg: var, cons, console, log)

  • Uses camel case notations. (eg: firstName, secondName)

  • JavaSceript is case sensitive.

                
                  let firstName = 'Thomas';
                  var lastName = 'Antony';
                  let ph_number = 9645611774;
                  const age = 10;
